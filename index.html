mershandlee is an architecture and design studio founded in 2017 in Vienna. Co-founded and co-run by Lida Badafareh and Mehrshad Atashi.

The studio explores spatial otherness through displacement, drawing, and research. We displace an object, drawing, or painting from its context and destabilize it until it resists full comprehension—producing "otherness." Recurring sources: Libeskind's Micromega, Lissitzky's Proun, Hejduk's theatrical settings and drawings.

The practice pivoted around 2023 from drawing and exhibition work toward civic, narrative, and ecological scales.

Teaching: Lida teaches at the Department for Architecture Theory and Philosophy of Technics (ATTP), TU Wien. Prior teaching at TU Graz, University of Innsbruck, and UdK Berlin.

Education: Lida holds an MA in Architecture from Städelschule, Frankfurt, and a PhD from University of Innsbruck (December 2024). Mehrshad studied at the University of Tehran.
